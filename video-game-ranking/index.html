Top 5 Video Games of All Time According to David

My Top 5 Video Games of All Time (In No Particular Order)

1. Donkey Kong Country 2: Diddy's Kong Quest

Year of release: 1995;
Platform(s) it released for: Super Nintendo Entertainment System;
Remakes, remasters or ports: Remade for the Game Boy Advance in 2005.

2. Super Mario Galaxy 2

Year of release: 2010;
Platform(s) it released for: Nintendo Wii;
Remakes, remasters or ports: None.

3. God Of War: Ragnarok

Year of release: 2022;
Platform(s) it released for: PlayStation 4; PlayStation 5;
Remakes, remasters or ports: None.

4. Donkey Kong Country: Tropical Freeze

Year of release: 2014;
Platform(s) it released for: Nintendo Wii U;
Remakes, remasters or ports: Ported to the Nintendo Switch in 2018.

5. Metroid Dread

Year of release: 2021;
Platform(s) it released for: Nintendo Switch;
Remakes, remasters or ports: None.
